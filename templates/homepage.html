Moving house or office involves genuine risk. Choosing a fully insured removals company in Birmingham ensures your belongings and property are protected from start to finish — with no compromise.

Public Liability Insurance

Our public liability removals cover protects against injury or property damage during your move.

  • ✓ Covers injury or property damage
  • ✓ Up to £5 million cover
  • ✓ Protects from unexpected accidents

Goods in Transit Insurance

Every item is covered under our goods in transit insurance throughout the entire removal journey.

  • ✓ Covers items during transport
  • ✓ Protection against theft, damage, or loss
  • ✓ Up to £50,000 cover

In the interest of full transparency, standard insurance does not cover the following:

How We Price Your Move

Every move is unique, which is why we do not publish fixed prices. The cost of your removal depends on several factors specific to your situation. What we do guarantee is complete transparency — the quote we provide is the price you pay. No hidden charges, no surprise extras on the day.

Factors That Affect Your Price

🏠

Property Size

The number of bedrooms and the overall volume of belongings are the primary factors. A studio flat will naturally cost less than a five-bedroom house.

📍

Distance

The distance between your current property and your new one affects the cost. Local Birmingham moves are typically more affordable than longer-distance relocations.

🚪

Access

Factors such as stairs, narrow hallways, long carry distances from van to door, and parking restrictions can all affect the time and effort required.

📦

Extra Services

Additional services such as professional packing, furniture dismantling, and temporary storage will be quoted separately and clearly itemised.

📅

Timing

Peak times such as end-of-month, Fridays and weekends may have different pricing due to higher demand. Midweek moves can often be more cost-effective.

🛋️

Special Items

Pianos, hot tubs, gym equipment, or other oversized items may require additional crew or specialist equipment, which will be clearly quoted in advance.

Our Pricing Promise

We believe in honest, transparent pricing. When you receive your quote from The Royals Removals, that is exactly what you will pay. We do not add fuel surcharges, stair charges, or any other hidden extras that some removal companies use to inflate the final bill.

If the scope of your move changes between the quote and moving day, we will discuss any adjustments with you in advance so there are never any surprises.

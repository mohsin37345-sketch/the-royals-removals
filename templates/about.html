About The Royals Removals

Birmingham's trusted premium removal company — built on care, honesty, and hard work.

Our Story

The Royals Removals was founded with a simple belief: moving home should not be stressful. Too many families in Birmingham were dealing with unreliable removal companies, hidden charges, and careless handling of their most valued possessions. We set out to change that.

Based in Small Heath, Birmingham, we have grown from a single van to a professional team serving the entire West Midlands. Our growth has come entirely through word-of-mouth recommendations and repeat customers — the best endorsement any business can receive.

What Drives Us

Every move tells a story. A young couple getting their first home together. A family upsizing for a new baby. A retired couple downsizing after decades in the same house. A business taking the next step with a bigger office. Behind every box and every piece of furniture is a life in transition, and we take that responsibility seriously.

Our team is trained not just in the physical aspects of removals — careful lifting, proper wrapping, efficient loading — but in customer service. We communicate clearly, arrive when we say we will, and treat your home with the same respect we would want shown to ours.
